Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct
Our Commitment

As contributors, maintainers, and community leaders, we are committed to creating a safe, inclusive, and harassment-free environment for everyone. We welcome participation from individuals of all backgrounds, regardless of age, body size, disability (visible or invisible), ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity or expression, experience level, education, socio-economic status, nationality, appearance, race, religion, or sexual orientation.

Our goal is to foster an open, respectful, and collaborative community where all members feel valued, supported, and empowered to contribute.

Community Standards
Expected Behavior

The following behaviors help maintain a healthy and productive community:

Demonstrating empathy, professionalism, and mutual respect

Respecting diverse viewpoints, experiences, and opinions

Giving and receiving constructive feedback gracefully

Taking responsibility for mistakes, offering sincere apologies, and learning from feedback

Acting in the best interest of the community as a whole

Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are strictly prohibited:

Sexualized language, imagery, or any form of unwanted sexual attention

Trolling, harassment, insulting remarks, or personal, political, or derogatory attacks

Harassment or intimidation in public or private spaces

Sharing private or sensitive information without explicit consent

Any conduct that is inappropriate, unprofessional, or harmful to the community

Enforcement Responsibilities

Community leaders are responsible for enforcing this Code of Conduct fairly and consistently. They have the authority to remove, edit, or reject content and contributions that violate these standards.

Whenever possible, moderation actions will be accompanied by clear explanations to maintain transparency and trust.

Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all community spaces, including online platforms, repositories, discussions, and offline events. It also applies when individuals are representing the community in official capacities, such as through emails, social media accounts, or organized events.

Reporting & Enforcement

Any form of abusive, harassing, or unacceptable behavior may be reported to designated community leaders. All reports will be handled promptly, confidentially, and with fairness.

Community leaders are committed to protecting the privacy, security, and well-being of those who report incidents.

Enforcement Guidelines

Consequences for violations will be determined based on severity, frequency, and impact:

Correction
Impact: Minor or unintentional violations
Action: A private written notice explaining the issue. A public acknowledgment or apology may be requested if appropriate.

Warning
Impact: Repeated or more serious misconduct
Action: A formal warning outlining expected behavior and potential consequences. Temporary restrictions may be applied.

Temporary Ban
Impact: Serious or ongoing violations
Action: Temporary suspension from community participation. Further violations may result in permanent removal.

Permanent Ban
Impact: Severe, continuous, or harmful behavior
Action: Permanent removal from all community spaces and activities.
